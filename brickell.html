Where To Eat

  • Bachour Bakery

    Antonio Bachour, a very famous chef, has opened this bakery and keeps surprising everyone with the most delicious desserts. It will be so hard to eat the desserts because they are very beautiful, like a piece of art. The entire food on the menu is unique and tastes amazing.

  • The Daily Creative Food Co.

    If you are very hungry and want to get some big meal, then the Daily Creative is your place. The portions are huge and very yummy. Get some American pancakes or an American breakfast (eggs, bacon, potatoes) and you'll be full till the end of the day.

  • All Day Miami

    All Day is a very hipster place with a great variety of coffee. You won't find a regular cappuccino on the menu but you can try something new and exciting. The design of "All Day" seems more like a coffee shop in San Francisco, so you will feel like you're in the other state, not Florida.
