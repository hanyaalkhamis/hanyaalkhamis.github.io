Where To Eat

  • Rosetta Bakery

    At Rosetta Bakery you can get some amazing pastries and Italian sandwiches. This place is not so cheap but the atmosphere there is great. You will feel like you're at the tiny bakery somewhere in Italy. Get ready for a big line because there is always many people there waiting for their freshly-baked goodies.

  • A la Folie Cafe

    It's a tiny French restaurant in the Spanish district of Miami Beach. You can get some «Pain au chocolat» and a cup of cappuccino while enjoying the quite and nice atmosphere. If you want to escape Miami and feel like you're somewhere in Europe that's the place you should go.

  • Visa-O1

    The best pizza place is definitely Visa-O1! It's called "a hidden pizza place" because it's located in the business center and it's pretty hard to find it. If you decide to get some pizza, then try "Star Luca" and "Marco". You won't be disappointed, I promise.
