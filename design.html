Where To Eat

  • Michael's Genuine Food & Drink

    If you like sharing your food then Michael's Geniune Food & Drink Restaurant is great because they serve tapas (small meals to share). Also, get a cup of coffee with a dessert and enjoy the atmosphere or Miami's weather if you're sitting outside and not afraid of the heat.

  • Harry's Pizzeria

    Shopping at the design district could get pretty tiring, so take a break and get some juicy pizza at the Harry's Pizzeria. You won't regret it. If you like New York's pizza then you'll enjoy Harry's.

  • Morgans

    You will feel like home when you're there. It's a two-story restaurant with a cozy interior design. If you are at the Morgans you should definitely get some eggs benedicts and a French toast with raspberry mascarpone filling. Sounds delicious, right? The meals are pretty big, so you won't leave hungry.
